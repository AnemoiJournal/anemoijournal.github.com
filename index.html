Anemoi: Journal of Pre-Modern Studies

A Scholarly Tradition

Anemoi, a scholarly journal produced by students under the sponsorship of Dr. David Rohrbacher at New College of Florida in Sarasota, will release its next issue in May 2017. Anemoi is dedicated to publishing original critical and analytical papers from all disciplines within Classics, Medieval and Renaissance, and Early Modern Studies. Because this journal seeks to provide a voice and publication opportunities for new scholars, only undergraduate work will be considered.

Submission

Submissions must concern a topic within Classics, Medieval and Renaissance, or Early Modern Studies. You may submit up to two manuscripts, but we will not accept more than one per person. All submissions must be formatted according to Chicago 16th Edition Documentary-Note Style (Footnotes).

Please visit our submission page for more details.
